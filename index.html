DebAssist

I created an app to help debaters, who do not have access to debate coaches, improve their speaking abilities. DebAssistant is an app to help and assist students in debate and public speaking as a whole. DebAssistant has two parts to it. First, it is a debate timer for primarily Public Forum Debate members as it allows them to time their speeches, crossfire, and prep times. The second section is an analysis section where debaters and other speakers can time their speeches and it will analyze them giving recommendations and feedback on the content and delivery of the speeches.
